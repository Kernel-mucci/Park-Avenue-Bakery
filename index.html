Our Story

A Quarter Century of Baking Excellence

Inspired by European patisseries, Park Avenue Bakery has been serving Helena with from-scratch breads and pastries for over 25 years. What started as a passion for traditional baking has grown into a beloved community gathering place.

Everything we make embodies our commitment to artisanal methods, premium ingredients, and delicious food. We rise before dawn every day to ensure our breads, pastries, and desserts are baked fresh using time-tested techniques.
